step1- Take the pizza dough and put in the centre.
step2- Apply some tomato sauce or puree on full pizza dough.
step3- For more cheesy pizza (before topping) apply some cheese slices.
step4- Top the pizza with all the chopped veggies(onions,capsicum,tomatoes,baby spinach,black or green olives) and corns.
step5- Don't forget to top the pizza with mozzarella cheese.
step6- Bake the pizza in the oven and if needed add some oregano or chilli flakes on it.
